Psychology

I'm a 4th year college student at the University of California, Riverside, on a mission to understand people better and help them lead happier lives. With a love for reading and a passion for psychology, I'm navigating my way through college with a clear goal in mind: to become a behavioral therapist. My journey into the realm of psychology began with a fascination for human behavior. I've always been intrigued by why people do the things they do and how their minds work. So, it was only natural for me to choose psychology as my major. College life is a whirlwind of lectures, assignments, and late-night study sessions, but I wouldn't have it any other way. Every class I take deepens my understanding of the human mind. Whether I'm learning about cognitive processes, social influences, or abnormal behavior, each lesson brings me closer to my dream career.

But it's not just about hitting the books all the time. One of my favorite ways to unwind is by diving into a good book. From gripping mysteries to heartwarming tales of love and friendship, I love them all. Reading not only helps me relax but also provides valuable insights into the human experience. It's like getting a crash course in empathy and understanding with every page I turn.As I progress through college, I'm constantly thinking about my future career as a behavioral therapist. I envision myself sitting across from clients, listening to their stories, and helping them overcome their challenges. The thought of being able to make a positive difference in someone's life fills me with excitement and purpose.

But I know that becoming a therapist isn't just about having a degree. It's about developing empathy, communication skills, and a deep understanding of human nature. That's why I'm always looking for ways to broaden my horizons and gain practical experience. Of course, the journey isn't always easy. There are moments of doubt and uncertainty, times when I wonder if I'm on the right path. But then I remember why I chose this field in the first place – to help others and make a meaningful impact in the world. So, I keep pushing forward, fueled by my passion for psychology and my desire to make a difference. With each passing day, I'm one step closer to achieving my dream of pursuing a career in behavioral therapy.
